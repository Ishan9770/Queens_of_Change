Who We Are

Born from the need for real change

Queens Of Change Foundation is an Indian Government registered NGO dedicated to uplifting communities through women empowerment, menstrual health education, and accessible learning. We believe every woman deserves dignity, knowledge, and opportunity — and we work tirelessly to make that a reality.

Founded by passionate changemakers, we partner with local communities, schools, and organizations to drive sustainable, ground-up transformation across India.

What We Do

Our Core Initiatives

♀️
Women Empowerment Programs
Skill development workshops, leadership training, and career guidance designed to give women the tools and confidence to lead independent, fulfilling lives.
🩸
Menstrual Health Awareness
Breaking the silence around menstrual health through community drives, free hygiene kit distribution, and school education programs that reduce stigma.
📚
Education for All
Providing free tutoring, scholarships, and digital literacy programs to ensure no girl is left behind due to financial or social barriers to education.
